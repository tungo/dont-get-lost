Find Your Friends!

No matter the distance, having Don't Get Lost on your phone means that your friends are always a click away! Being able to see their location in real time makes it all the easier to become their navigator from afar.

Stay Connected

Don't Get Lost allows users to create groups. The app renders all members for a given group at a time. Users can choose which group to render on the map.

How It Works

Don't Get Lost integrates Airbnb's AirMapView and React Native's built-in geolocation api to render the locations of all group members on demand. The app captures the user's longitude and latitude, automatically updating their position overtime.

Relevant user information is stored in a PostgreSQL database, which is retrieved through RESTful calls to its Rails backend.
